About

Connecting individuals involved in Trap-Neuter-Return(TNR) enabling them access and offer essential services.

Trapperaid is a platform that centralizes services (funds, holding space, transport) while providing the local governments an access to the data about the numbers of spayed/neutered cats, their treatment cost, and veterinary offices that offer discounted services to feral cats.

Every person willing to help feral cats can join Trapperaid!
